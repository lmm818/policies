site content
what it says:
when you share, post, or upload content that is covered by intellectual property rights (like photos or videos) on or in connection with our service, you hereby grant to us a non-exclusive, royalty-free, transferable, sub-licensable, worldwide license to host, use, distribute, modify, run, copy, publicly perform or display, translate, and create derivative works of your content (consistent with your privacy and application settings).
what it means:
you keep all the rights to the posts your upload on instagram, but you give instagram some rights in order for them to host and promote your posts.
what it says:
you give us permission to show your username, profile picture, and information about your actions (such as likes) or relationships (such as follows) next to or in connection with accounts, ads, offers, and other sponsored content that you follow or engage with that are displayed on facebook products, without any compensation to you.
what it means:
businesses or people who promote posts may see the actions you take on their advertisements such as likes on a post.
using instagram
what it says:
how you can't use instagram. providing a safe and open service for a broad community requires that we all do our part.
what it means:
instagram has a list of behaviors that go against their guidelines. they include but are not limited to: impersonation, illegal activities, violating terms, interfering with instagram's services, collecting information in unauthorized ways, and posting others' private or confidential information.
what it says:
we can refuse to provide or stop providing all or part of the service to you (including terminating or disabling your account) immediately to protect our community or services, or if you create risk or legal exposure for us, violate these terms of dse or our policies, if you repeatedly infringe other people's intellectual property rights, or where we are permitted or required to do so by law.
what it means:
if you do not follow the community guidelines given by instagram or their policies, instagram has a right to terminate your account and discontinue services with you.
privacy policy
key takeaways:
– instagram collects information such as images and content you provide, how you engage with the platform, transactions you have made, things others do to provide information about you such as tagged pictures.
– instagram also learns the people, pages, hashtags you follow and how you interact with them.
– device information such as operating system, hardware system, device signals (bluetooth, wifi, cell phone towers), data your device allows, and networks.
– business partners provide facebook information of yours such as a business could tell instagram about a purchase you made in its store.
– instagram uses this information to personalize your experience with them and improve their products. they also use it to get business analytics, promote safety, and for social research.
