privacy policy
key takeaways:
– tiktok collects information needed to create an account, information shared from third party social networks, how you use the platform (likes, swipes), messages sent through the platform, and contacts (if applicable).
– tiktok collects information about the device you use the application on including IP address, model of device, mobile carrier, time zone, screen resolution, operating system, app and file names and types, and keystroke patterns or rhythms.
– tiktok collects information about your location based on your SIM card, IP address or GPS location.
– tiktok scans and analyzes messages sent on the platform including things such as composing the message, sending, receiving, content of the message, when it was sent, received, read, and those participating in the conversation.
– the above is collected to send you customized ads, improve the “for you page” to your tastes, infer information about you, allow for you to interact with the content, detect fraud, and improve algorithms.
– your information may be shared with business partners, within tiktok’s corporation, and for legal purposes.
– you may limit the above by opting out targeted advertising in your phone settings, opt out of emails, switch off GPS, and review the information you have already given tiktok.
